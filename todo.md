**How this works:**
- Pass this file at the start of every relevant handoff.
- New out-of-scope or deferred items are added as they arise.
- Completed and cancelled items are moved to `todo-archive.md` at close.
- Numbers are unique IDs only — not sequential priority.
- Add new items to the top and increment from the current highest ID.

**Status tags:**
- **[NEXT-UP]** — ready for future execution, no known blocker
- **[PENDING]** — blocked by an external dependency, decision, or prerequisite
- **[FROZEN]** — intentionally paused until David raises it
- **[WIP]** — being worked on right now
- **[DONE]** — complete; move to `todo-archive.md` at close
- **[CANCELLED]** — will not be done; move to `todo-archive.md` at close

**Group tags:**
- **[ADR]** — architecture decision records and conversion logic
- **[AM1]** — Anvil technical/specification documents
- **[MM1]** — Mybizz conceptual/design documents
- **[AM2]** — working/design Anvil documents
- **[MM2]** — working/design Mybizz documents
- **[PROMPTS]** — prompts, build-rules, implementation instructions
- **[VALIDATION]** — cross-checking, mismatch review, provenance checks
- **[DEVLOG]** — devlog system, handoff records, tracking consistency
- **[TOOLING]** — local tooling, config, scripts, workflow support
- **[DAVID]** — action required by David outside the docs
